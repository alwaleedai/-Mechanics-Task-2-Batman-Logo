# -Mechanics-Task-2-Batman-Logo
he **Batman logo** was selected from the image. Its contour was extracted, measured in pixels, and then converted into real-world millimeter dimensions.

## Final Dimensions
- Overall width: **60 mm**
- Height: **≈ 25.6 mm**
- Part thickness (Extrude): **2 mm**
- Circular hole diameter (key/wrench entry): **4 mm** — placed at the center of the logo body (the thickest, most solid area) to avoid breaking the part during printing.

## Steps Followed in Onshape
1. Import the logo image (Import Image) as a Sketch background.
2. Trace the outer outline over the image using the Spline/Line tool.
3. Create a 4 mm diameter circle in the solid central area.
4. Extrude the entire part to a thickness of 2 mm.
5. Export the model as STL.
